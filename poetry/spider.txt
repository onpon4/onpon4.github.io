Steadily weave away
Again to see the day
Delicate work of art
Only to come apart
Moulded into a shape
Affixed just like a drape
Still to await its fate
Or leave them to escape
Change the strings again
Hate to see this end
In confidence, you stand
Swift to take command
Meeting them as they land

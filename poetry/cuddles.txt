A warmly soothing embrace
a dream unlike I've faced
into a blissful romance
dream made real by your hands
as I itch to stay herefore
yours now and forevermore
amiss, a feeling not had
aligned next to you I am glad
no dream can ever replace
nestling, ever in your embrace.

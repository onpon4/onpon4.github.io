Witches' Hunt

This is a tactical scenario for MegaGlest. You can install it by extracting the Zip file below to ~/.megaglest/scenarios (or wherever scenarios for your MegaGlest installation are located).

  • Download: witches_hunt.zip
  • Forum Thread: https://forum.megaglest.org/index.php?topic=9953.0

Help a group of witches use their vast army of minions to destroy seven enemy bases. Along the way, defend the witches from enemies that roam the map.

  • Player faction: Magic
  • Enemy faction: Indian, Magic, Tech, Egypt
  • Tileset: Dark Forest
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Engine: MegaGlest
